About us

Federation of Training and Placement Officers (FTPO) is a group which is formed by the Training and Placement Officers of Pune District in 2007.

Major Objectives of this group:
1. To create a common platform for Industries and Institutes.
2. To improve and re design the Training and Placement Processes.
3. To increase the job opportunities for students.
4. Addressing the common issues and concerns of the member TPO.
5. Networking among Engineering Institutes.
6. Bringing equal opportunities to member colleges in terms of jobs, MoUs , Projects and internships.
